Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) Jobs

ICE Officers conducting field operations

ICE offers diverse career opportunities in law enforcement, immigration operations, and homeland security:

  • Deportation Officers

    Enforce immigration laws and manage cases of individuals subject to removal from the United States.

  • Criminal Investigators

    Conduct complex criminal investigations related to border security, customs violations, and immigration fraud.

  • Intelligence Research Specialists

    Analyze data and provide tactical intelligence support for ICE operations and investigations.

  • Mission Support Specialists

    Provide critical administrative and technical support to facilitate ICE operations.

Most positions require U.S. citizenship and the ability to obtain security clearance.

Intelligence Careers

Modern Intelligence Operations Center

Intelligence agencies offer challenging roles crucial to national security:

  • Intelligence Analysts

    Analyze complex data to identify patterns and trends related to national security threats.

  • Cybersecurity Specialists

    Protect critical information systems and networks from cyber threats.

  • Foreign Language Experts

    Provide critical translation and analysis of foreign communications.

  • Technical Collection Officers

    Operate sophisticated systems to gather intelligence information.

Positions typically require advanced degrees, security clearance, and specialized training.
