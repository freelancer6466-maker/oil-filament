Senior Benefits in Canada

Comprehensive Guide to Canadian Senior Benefits and Pensions

Old Age Security (OAS)

Basic OAS Pension

Up to $642.25/month

For seniors aged 65 and older

  • Automatic enrollment at 65
  • Based on years in Canada
  • Income-tested benefit

Guaranteed Income Supplement

Up to $959.26/month

Additional support for low-income seniors

  • Must receive OAS
  • Income-based eligibility
  • Monthly payments

Allowance Benefits

Up to $1,219.68/month

For spouses/partners aged 60-64

  • Income-tested
  • Must live in Canada
  • Partner receives OAS & GIS

Canada Pension Plan (CPP)

Retirement Pension

Average $737.13/month

Based on contributions during working years

Post-Retirement Benefit

Additional monthly amount

For those who work while receiving CPP

Disability Benefits

Up to $1,457.45/month

For CPP contributors with disabilities

Eligibility Requirements

1

Age Requirements

  • 65+ for OAS
  • 60+ for CPP retirement pension
  • 60-64 for Allowance
2

Residency

  • Legal resident/citizen
  • 10+ years in Canada for OAS
  • Current Canadian resident
3

Income Thresholds

  • OAS recovery tax threshold
  • GIS income limits
  • Allowance income tests
