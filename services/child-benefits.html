Child Benefits in Canada

Supporting Canadian Families with Children

Canada Child Benefit (CCB)

Base Amount

Up to $6,997

Per year for each child under 6

Child 6-17

Up to $5,903

Per year for each child aged 6 through 17

Additional Benefits

  • Child disability benefit
  • Provincial benefits
  • Territory supplements

Eligibility Requirements

  • Live with the child who is under 18 years of age
  • Primary person responsible for child's care
  • Canadian resident for tax purposes
  • You or your spouse must be:
    • Canadian citizen
    • Permanent resident
    • Protected person
    • Temporary resident (living in Canada for 18 months)

How to Apply

1

Register Your Child

Register the birth of your child with your province or territory

2

Automatic Enrollment

If you've registered the birth, you may be automatically enrolled

3

Manual Application

If not automatically enrolled, apply through:

  • My Account on CRA website
  • Form RC66 Canada Child Benefits Application
4

Supporting Documents

Provide required documentation:

  • Proof of birth
  • Proof of residency
  • Status in Canada
