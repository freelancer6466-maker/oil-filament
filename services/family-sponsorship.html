Family Sponsorship

Reunite with Your Loved Ones in Canada

Who Can You Sponsor?

Spouse or Partner

  • Legal spouse
  • Common-law partner
  • Conjugal partner

Dependent Children

  • Under 22 years old
  • Full-time students
  • Financially dependent

Parents & Grandparents

  • Annual invitation process
  • Income requirements
  • 20-year commitment

Sponsor Requirements

Financial Requirements

  • Meet minimum income requirements
  • Provide financial documents
  • Sign undertaking agreement

Eligibility Criteria

  • Be 18 years or older
  • Be a Canadian citizen or permanent resident
  • Live in Canada (some exceptions apply)

Application Process

  • Confirm eligibility (both sponsor and sponsored person)
  • Gather required documents
  • Complete application forms
  • Pay sponsorship and processing fees
  • Submit complete application package
  • Respond to requests for additional information
  • Attend interviews if required

Processing Times

Spouses & Partners

12 months standard processing

Dependent Children

12 months standard processing

Parents & Grandparents

20-24 months standard processing
