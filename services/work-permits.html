Work Permits

Your Path to Working in Canada

Types of Work Permits

Open Work Permit

Allows you to work for any employer in Canada

  • No job offer required
  • Work for any employer
  • Change jobs freely

Employer-Specific Work Permit

Tied to a specific employer in Canada

  • Valid job offer required
  • LMIA may be needed
  • Specific location and role

Key Requirements

  • Prove you'll leave Canada when your permit expires
  • Show you have enough money to support yourself
  • Have no criminal record
  • Be in good health (medical exam may be required)
  • Not plan to work for ineligible employers
  • Have valid job offer (for employer-specific permits)

Popular Programs

PGWP

Post-Graduation Work Permit

  • For international graduates
  • Up to 3 years duration
  • Open work permit

TFWP

Temporary Foreign Worker Program

  • Employer must have LMIA
  • Specific job offer needed
  • Wage requirements apply

IMP

International Mobility Program

  • LMIA-exempt
  • Based on broader economic benefit
  • Includes youth mobility

Application Process

  • Determine your eligibility and work permit type
  • Gather required documents
  • Complete application forms
  • Pay application fees
  • Submit biometrics if required
  • Wait for processing
