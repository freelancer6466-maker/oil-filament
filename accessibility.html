Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: September 4, 2025

Our Commitment

oil-filament is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

We strive to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, which include:

  • Proper heading structure and navigation
  • Alt text for all meaningful images
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Keyboard accessibility
  • Screen reader compatibility

Accessibility Features

Navigation

  • Skip to main content link
  • Consistent navigation structure
  • ARIA landmarks and labels
  • Visible focus indicators

Content

  • Readable font sizes and styles
  • Text resizing without loss of functionality
  • Alternative text for images
  • Descriptive link text

Forms

  • Clear form labels and instructions
  • Error identification and suggestions
  • Keyboard-accessible form controls

Assistive Technologies

Our website supports various assistive technologies, including:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
  • Speech recognition software
  • Screen magnification tools
  • Alternative input devices

Known Issues

We are working to resolve the following accessibility issues:

  • Some legacy PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Third-party content may not meet all accessibility standards
  • Complex data visualizations may require alternative formats

Feedback and Support

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. Please contact us if you:

  • Encounter any accessibility barriers
  • Need assistance accessing any content
  • Have suggestions for improvement
