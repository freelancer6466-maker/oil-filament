Patent Examiner

Full Time
Washington, DC
Patent and Trademark Office
$72,750 - $141,611 per year
Closes Sep 30, 2025

Overview

The Patent and Trademark Office (PTO) is seeking a Patent Examiner to join our innovative team. As a Patent Examiner, you will play a crucial role in examining patent applications and determining whether patents should be granted for claimed inventions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Review and analyze patent applications in computer architecture, software, and cybersecurity fields
  • Evaluate innovations for patentability under US patent law
  • Conduct thorough prior art searches using various databases and resources
  • Write clear and comprehensive office actions explaining examination findings
  • Communicate with patent attorneys and inventors regarding applications
  • Stay current with technological advances in the field

Qualifications

Required:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related field
  • U.S. citizenship
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication abilities

Preferred:

  • Advanced degree in related field
  • Experience with patent law or intellectual property
  • Knowledge of USPTO practices and procedures

Benefits

  • Competitive salary with regular increases
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Federal Employee Retirement System (FERS)
  • Thrift Savings Plan (TSP) with matching contributions
  • Paid vacation and sick leave
  • Flexible work schedules and telework options
  • Professional development and training opportunities
